Leadership Team

The people behind Brother's Place — called by Christ, rooted in Rome, GA.

Founders

TK & Claudia Hamilton

📷 Photo of TK & Claudia Hamilton

TK and Claudia Hamilton are both natives of Rome, Georgia, with deep, lifelong ties to the recovery community in Floyd County. Through their own journey of overcoming hardship through faith in Christ, they were called to create a place where men facing homelessness, addiction, and brokenness could find the same hope they found.

Together they founded Brother's Place, bringing together their passion for community, their roots in Northwest Georgia, and their conviction that every man deserves a chance to rebuild his life with dignity and the love of Christ.

TK and Claudia are dedicated to walking alongside individuals on their journey to recovery, providing a safe and supportive environment where faith, accountability, and community intersect.

Executive Director

Maloy Owen

📷 Photo of Maloy Owen

Maloy Owen serves as Executive Director of Brother's Place, bringing a unique blend of international training, local roots, and pastoral heart to the role.

Trained in Zambia and certified in Advanced Missions for community revival, Maloy has 5 years of experience leading another nonprofit dedicated to serving the same community Brother's Place serves today. He has 3 years of pastoral service and thousands of volunteer hours invested in the lives of men and families in Floyd County.

His heart has always been to connect deeply with others and battle alongside them to reach their goals. Under Maloy's leadership, Brother's Place continues to grow as a trusted resource for men in recovery across Northwest Georgia.

Email: maloy@brothersplace.org
