Who Leads Brother's Place.

Called by Christ, rooted in Rome, Georgia, and committed to walking with men through every stage of recovery.

A Team Built for This Work

Brother's Place is led by people who have walked the road they ask others to walk. Our founders are natives of Rome with personal recovery experience. Our executive director has spent years pastoring and leading nonprofits in this same community. Our board provides governance, accountability, and prayer cover for the work. Together, this team is the engine of the ministry.

Founders and Executive Director

The people leading Brother's Place day to day.

TH

TK Hamilton

Co-Founder

A native of Rome, Georgia with deep ties to the local recovery community. TK's own journey through hardship and faith in Christ shaped the heart of Brother's Place. He walks alongside residents in daily life, accountability, and prayer.

CH

Claudia Hamilton

Co-Founder

Claudia is a Rome native and lifelong advocate for families touched by addiction. She co-founded Brother's Place to make sure men in crisis, and the families who love them, would have a Christ-centered place to land.

MO

Maloy Owen

Executive Director

Maloy is executive director of Brothers Place. He trained in Zambia and received certification in Advanced Missions to bring community revival. He has 5 years of experience leading another non-profit dedicated to serving the same community we serve now. He has three years of pastoral service as well as thousands of volunteer hours. His heart has always been to connect deeply with others and battle alongside them to reaching their goals. maloy@brothersplace.org

Board of Directors

Our board provides governance, accountability, and strategic direction for Brother's Place. Board members serve voluntarily and review program outcomes, financial stewardship, and partnerships on a regular cadence.

Board roster and bios are being finalized for public release. If you are interested in serving on the Brother's Place board or learning more about our governance, please reach out.
