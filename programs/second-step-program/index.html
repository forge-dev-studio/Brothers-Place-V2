Second Step Program in Rome, GA

Structured, Christ-centered transitional housing and recovery classes for men after rehab in Northwest Georgia.

What Is a Second Step Program?

A second step program is structured transitional housing for people who have completed detox or inpatient rehab. It bridges the gap between intensive treatment and independent living.

Residents live in a safe, substance-free home while attending recovery classes, working with a case manager, and rebuilding the daily routines that support long-term sobriety.

Unlike standard sober living, a second step program provides structured programming — mandatory classes, spiritual formation, job training, and accountability — designed to address the root causes of addiction and prevent relapse.

How Brother's Place's Second Step Program Works

Admission

Men who have completed an inpatient rehabilitation program, detox program, or who are actively in recovery and demonstrate a commitment to sobriety are eligible. We welcome men of any faith background. Apply through our online application or call 706.509.9020.

Length of Stay

The typical program length is 6 to 12 months, individualized to each man's needs and progress. We don't rush the process — lasting recovery takes time.

Daily & Weekly Structure

Residents follow a structured weekly schedule that includes recovery classes, Bible study, case management meetings, job training sessions, house responsibilities, and community meals. Mornings begin with devotion and end with evening check-ins.

What Makes It Christ-Centered

Faith is the foundation of our program — not as a requirement for admission, but as the source of lasting transformation. Bible studies, worship, and pastoral mentoring are woven into the weekly rhythm. Every man is invited to explore a relationship with Christ at his own pace. We believe spiritual healing is essential to recovery that lasts.

Cost

We work with each resident individually. Our goal is to remove every barrier to recovery. Contact us to discuss your situation — we will never turn a man away solely because of finances.

Second Step vs. Other Recovery Options

Understanding where a second step program fits in the recovery journey:

Program Type Duration Structure Level Best For
Detox 3–7 days Medical / Clinical Immediate medical stabilization
Inpatient Rehab 28–90 days High (residential) Intensive therapy and initial recovery
Second Step Program ✦ 6–12 months Moderate–High Structured transition after rehab — classes, jobs, faith
Sober Living Flexible Low–Moderate Substance-free housing with peer support
Independent Living Ongoing Self-directed Full independence post-recovery

Classes Included in the Program

Our second step program includes structured recovery, life-skills, and faith-based classes taught on a weekly schedule:

🛡️ Relapse Prevention

Evidence-informed strategies for identifying triggers, building coping skills, and maintaining sobriety long-term.

📖 Faith-Based Recovery

Biblical principles applied to the recovery journey. Exploring how faith in Christ provides the ultimate foundation for healing.

💼 Job Readiness & Resume

Resume building, interview preparation, workplace skills, and connections with local employers in Floyd County.

💰 Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, credit repair, and financial planning — building the skills for long-term independence.

Outcomes

Since opening our doors in 2023, Brother's Place has served over 47 men through our programs. While we are a young ministry, we are committed to measuring our impact transparently:

  • Men who complete the program demonstrate significantly improved housing stability
  • Alumni report stronger faith foundations and community connections
  • Employment placement support has connected graduates with local employers across Floyd County

Detailed outcome data will be published in our first annual report. View our financials page for current transparency information.
