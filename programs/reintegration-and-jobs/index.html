Reintegration and Jobs

Job training, employment placement, and a permanent housing plan for men finishing the program.

Recovery That Holds in the Real World

The day a man leaves Brother's Place is not the end of the work. It is the test of it. Sobriety holds when there is a paycheck on Friday, a key to a place of his own, and a community on the other side of the door. That is what reintegration is for.

Our reintegration and jobs track is built around one outcome. Every brother who completes the program walks out with a resume, real interview experience, an employer in Rome or Floyd County, and a written plan for permanent housing. If a man needs his GED, we help him earn it. If he needs financial literacy, we teach it. If he needs help opening a bank account, we go with him.

This is not a class. It is a coordinated push, owned by his case manager and worked on every week of his stay, so that the day he walks out is the day his independent life begins.

What We Help You Build

Practical, hands-on preparation for the working world and the life that comes after Brother's Place.

Resume and Work History

A clean, honest resume that tells a man's story without hiding it. Built one-on-one, then refined through real applications.

Interview Skills

Mock interviews, practical coaching on how to talk about a hard past, and rehearsal until the first real interview feels familiar.

Employment Placement

Warm hand-offs to local employer partners across Rome and Floyd County who are ready to give a man in recovery a real shot.

Permanent Housing Plan

A written plan for after Brother's Place. Voucher applications, savings goals, references, and the first month rent strategy.

Local Employer Partners

Brother's Place works with employers across Rome and Floyd County who are willing to interview and hire men in recovery. These are real businesses with real openings in the trades, manufacturing, food service, hospitality, warehousing, and retail.

We do not list partner names publicly out of respect for our employers and our brothers. If you are a hiring manager in Northwest Georgia and want to connect with motivated candidates who are working a recovery program, please reach out to us directly. If you are a brother applying for residency, your case manager will introduce you to the partners that match your background and goals.
