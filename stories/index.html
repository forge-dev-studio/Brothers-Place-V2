Stories from Brother's Place.

Real men have walked through these doors and rebuilt their lives. Here is one of their stories, in his own words, with more to come as residents and alumni choose to share them.

Why Stories Matter

Numbers tell part of the truth, but transformation has a face and a name. Behind every bed, every meal, and every Bible study is a man who walked in carrying everything he owned in a trash bag and walked out a year later with a paycheck, a key to his own apartment, and a brotherhood that still calls every Sunday.

Every story shared here is real and shared with permission. More will be added as residents and alumni choose to tell theirs.

Testimony

A Story From the House

What I loved about Brother's Place is that they treated me like a grown man. I've been in a lot of programs and they always made me feel like I was a kid who didn't know any better. Not Brother's Place. They made me feel like a man, like I was capable, and could do this. I really think that is why I made it.

Jerry R.

Brother's Place Alumnus
