Privacy, Terms & Accessibility

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Brother's Place ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, donors, volunteers, and residents. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit brothersplace.org.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website:

  • Name, email address, phone number (when submitted via forms)
  • Donation information (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Website usage data via Google Analytics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process donations and send tax receipts
  • To respond to inquiries and applications
  • To send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and services

Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Donation processing is handled by secure, PCI-compliant payment processors. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS.

Accessibility Statement

Brother's Place is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We strive to meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards across our website.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML5 structure throughout
  • Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
  • Color contrast ratios meeting 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text
  • Visible focus indicators on all interactive elements
  • Descriptive alt text on all meaningful images
  • ARIA labels where appropriate
  • Skip-to-content link on every page
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website, please contact us at maloy@brothersplace.org or 706.509.9020. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will work to resolve any issues promptly.
