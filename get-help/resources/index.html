Other Resources in Northwest Georgia.

If Brother's Place is not the right fit right now, here are friends and partners who can help.

Community Over Competition

Recovery is too important for turf wars. If our home is not the right next step for you, the people on this page are. We have walked alongside many of these organizations and we trust them. Call us first if you want help thinking through where to start, we will be honest about who we think can serve you best.

Crisis and Safety

If you are in immediate danger or someone you love is, start here.

988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential, 24/7 support for people in distress. Call or text.

Call or text 988

SAMHSA National Helpline

Free, confidential treatment referral and information service for individuals and families dealing with mental health or substance use disorders.

1-800-662-HELP (4357)

Georgia Crisis and Access Line

Statewide behavioral health crisis line, available 24/7 for Georgians in mental health or substance use crisis.

1-800-715-3577

Emergency, 911

If you are in a life-threatening medical or safety emergency, call 911 immediately.

Call 911
