Apply for Residency at Brother's Place

Take the first step toward a new chapter. We're ready to walk with you.

Before You Apply

Our application takes about 10–15 minutes. Here's what to expect after you submit:

  1. We'll review your application within 1–2 business days
  2. A team member will call you for a brief screening conversation
  3. If approved, we'll schedule your intake at the Maple Street home
  4. You'll begin your first 30 days of stability, community, and growth

Prefer to talk to someone directly? Call 706.509.9020
