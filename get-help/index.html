You Don't Have to Walk This Road Alone

If you're struggling with homelessness, addiction, or just feeling lost — Brother's Place is here for you. Right here in Rome, Georgia. Today.

Apply for Residency 📞 Call 706.509.9020

What to Expect

From your first call to your first month with us — here's how the journey unfolds.

1. Apply Online or Call

Fill out our short residency application through Sobriety Hub, or call us directly at 706.509.9020. We respond to every inquiry.

2. Screening Conversation

Our team will have a phone or in-person conversation with you to understand your situation, answer your questions, and determine if Brother's Place is the right fit.

3. Intake & Welcome

If accepted, you'll come to our Maple Street home for intake. We'll help you settle in, introduce you to the community, and develop your personalized plan.

4. Your First 30 Days

The first month focuses on stability — safe housing, consistent meals, initial case management, and beginning to build the routines that support recovery. We walk with you every step.

What You'll Receive

🏠 Safe, Stable Housing

A clean, dignified room in our Maple Street home with meals, shared living spaces, and a community of men supporting each other.

🤝 Personal Case Management

A dedicated case manager who helps you navigate services, set goals, and connect with local agencies for wrap-around support.

✝️ Spiritual Community

Bible studies, worship, mentorship, and fellowship — offered with love, not as a requirement but as a path to deeper healing.

🎯 Job Training & Placement

Help building your resume, preparing for interviews, and connecting with local employers who want to give you a chance.

In Crisis Right Now?

If you need immediate help, call us at 706.509.9020. If you're in a life-threatening emergency, call 911.

If Brother's Place isn't the right fit, we can help connect you with other resources in the Rome and Floyd County area. View local resources →
