Four Pillars of Restoration

Every man who walks through our doors receives comprehensive, Christ-centered support designed to rebuild his life from the ground up.

Supportive Housing

Safe, stable living at our Maple Street home for men facing homelessness, addiction, or mental-health struggles.

Learn more

Case Management

Community case management and linkage to wrap-around services to help men overcome every barrier they face.

Learn more

Spiritual Formation

Christ-centered discipleship, mentoring, and worship that nourishes the soul and builds lasting faith.

Learn more

Reintegration

Job training, employment placement, and permanent-housing support to transition men back into independence.

Learn more

Honest Work Restores a Man.

Job-skills training and apprenticeship rebuild more than a paycheck. They rebuild dignity, rhythm, and the quiet pride a man earns by making something with his hands.

Every man at Brother's Place leaves with a trade, a reference, and a way back into the workforce.
