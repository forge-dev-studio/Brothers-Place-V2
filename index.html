Rome, Georgia

Men's Faith Based Recovery.

Brother's Place is Christ-centered supportive housing, recovery, and brotherhood for men in Rome, Georgia walking the road of reintegration after detox.

Our mission is to empower, equip, and enrich the lives of men through the love of Jesus Christ.

Donate Get Help

What Brother's Place Is

A Christ-centered second-step home in Rome, Georgia for men rebuilding their lives after detox — typically 6 months to 2 years. Three meals, a bed, a community of brothers, and the steady work of getting back on your feet.

Rooted on Maple Street in Rome and walking with men across Floyd County and the surrounding region.

How to Apply

Four Pillars of Restoration

Every man who walks through our doors receives comprehensive, Christ-centered support designed to rebuild his life from the ground up.

Supportive Housing

Safe, stable living at our Maple Street home for men facing homelessness, addiction, or mental-health struggles.

Learn more

Case Management

Community case management and linkage to wrap-around services to help men overcome every barrier they face.

Learn more

Spiritual Formation

Christ-centered discipleship, mentoring, and worship that nourishes the soul and builds lasting faith.

Learn more

Reintegration

Job training, employment placement, and permanent-housing support to transition men back into independence.

Learn more

Job Skills and Reintegration

We help men learn a trade, find work, and move into permanent housing. Job training happens with mentors who've done the same work themselves.

Most men are working before they leave. The transition to independence begins here, not after.
