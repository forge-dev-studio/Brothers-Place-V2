Four Pillars of Restoration

Every man who walks through our doors receives comprehensive, Christ-centered support designed to rebuild his life from the ground up.

Supportive Housing

Safe, stable living at our Maple Street home for men facing homelessness, addiction, or mental-health struggles.

Learn more

Case Management

Community case management and linkage to wrap-around services to help men overcome every barrier they face.

Learn more

Spiritual Formation

Christ-centered discipleship, mentoring, and worship that nourishes the soul and builds lasting faith.

Learn more

Reintegration

Job training, employment placement, and permanent-housing support to transition men back into independence.

Learn more

A Journey of Hope

"You led them with a pillar of cloud by day, and with a pillar of fire by night, to illuminate the way they should go."

Nehemiah 9:12

At Brother's Place, we believe the teachings and love of Jesus Christ are the foundation of transformation and renewal. This is not just a place to stay. It is a community where the love of Christ guides everything we do. Through Jesus' example, we offer guidance, compassion, and empowerment to help men in Rome, Georgia rebuild their lives.
