Donate

Your gift pays for housing, meals, and the day-to-day work of helping men in Rome, Georgia rebuild their lives.

Where Your Gift Goes

Every dollar you give to Brother's Place stays in Rome, Georgia and supports a man in recovery. Your gift funds housing at our Maple Street home, three meals a day, case management, recovery programming, and the brotherhood that makes lasting change possible. We are a 501(c)(3) and 92 percent of donations go directly to resident services. See our financial transparency page for the full breakdown.

Choose Your Impact

Pick a monthly level that fits your means. Every tier directly funds a man's recovery at Brother's Place.

Other Ways to Give

Online giving is one path. Here are two more.

Mail a Check

Make checks payable to Brother's Place and mail to 3 Central Plaza #230, Rome, GA 30165. Every check is acknowledged with a tax receipt.

Stock and Estate Gifts

Stock and estate gifts can be coordinated by emailing maloy@brothersplace.org. We work with each donor's advisor to make these gifts simple.

Your Gift is Tax-Deductible

Brother's Place is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. EIN available on request. You will receive a written acknowledgment for any gift of $250 or more for your records.

Support Brother's Place

Donations stay in Rome and pay for housing, food, transportation, and the work of helping men get back on their feet. Every dollar matters.
