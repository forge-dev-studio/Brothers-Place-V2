Volunteer Opportunities in Rome, GA

You have something valuable to offer. Here's how you can serve men in recovery at Brother's Place.

How You Can Help

Whether you have a few hours a week or one Saturday a month, there's a meaningful role for you.

🍳

Meal Preparation

Help prepare and serve meals for residents. Great for individuals, families, or church groups looking for a hands-on way to serve.

When: Mornings, evenings, or weekends

🤝

Mentoring & Fellowship

Spend time with residents — share a meal, play cards, have a conversation. Your presence and encouragement matter more than you know.

When: Flexible, evenings preferred

📖

Bible Study & Worship

Lead or co-lead a Bible study, devotional, or worship session. Help ground men in God's Word during a pivotal time in their lives.

When: Weekday evenings

👔

Job Readiness Coaching

Help residents build resumes, practice interviews, navigate job searches, and prepare for the workplace. Business professionals especially welcome.

When: By appointment

📦

Donation Drives

Organize a clothing drive, food drive, or hygiene-product collection at your church, business, or school. We'll provide the wish list.

When: Anytime

🔨

Property & Maintenance

Help with lawn care, minor repairs, cleaning, painting, or general upkeep of the Maple Street home. Handy volunteers are always needed.

When: Weekends or by arrangement
