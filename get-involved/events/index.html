Events.

Gatherings, fundraisers, and moments to meet the men of Brother's Place.

The Rhythm of Our Year

Brother's Place gathers throughout the year for fundraisers, retreats, and church-hosted prayer breakfasts. These are not just events. They are how our community sees the work, hears from the men, and stands shoulder to shoulder with us. Come once and you will likely come back. Bring a friend, a pastor, or your small group.

Annual Events

The three gatherings that anchor our year. Dates announced as 2026 calendar firms up.

Annual Banquet

Fall Fundraiser

Our flagship event. Dinner, testimonies from current and former residents, and the chance to invest in next year's work. Tables for sponsors, individual seats welcome.

Email maloy@brothersplace.org for next date.

Men's Retreat

Spring Weekend

A weekend off the property for residents, alumni, mentors, and partner-church men. Worship, teaching, fellowship, and rest. The most spiritually formative weekend of our year.

Email maloy@brothersplace.org for next date.

Prayer Breakfast

Monthly, Rotating Hosts

Partner churches across Floyd County take turns hosting a Saturday prayer breakfast. Pastors, mentors, and friends of Brother's Place gather to pray over the men by name.

Email maloy@brothersplace.org for next date.

"Standing in front of that banquet room, telling my story, knowing my mom was in the audience, knowing the man across the table believed in me when nobody else did. That night changed something in me."

A Brother's Place Resident, Annual Banquet
