Church Partnerships in Rome, GA & Northwest Georgia

Your congregation can be the hands and feet of Christ for men rebuilding their lives.

Dear Pastor,

Brother's Place exists because of the local church. We were founded by church members, led by a man with pastoral experience, and sustained by congregations across Floyd County who believe that caring for "the least of these" is not optional — it's the heart of the Gospel.

We invite your church to walk alongside us in this ministry. There are several ways your congregation can partner with Brother's Place, and we'll work with you to find the right fit for your church's gifts, capacity, and calling.

Partnership Tiers

  • ✓ Add Brother's Place to your church prayer list
