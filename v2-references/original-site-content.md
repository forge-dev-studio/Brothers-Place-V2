## Programs Offered (exact names and one-line descriptions)

The original site does NOT list discrete program pages. It uses four pillars repeated on home and [/what-we-do](https://www.brothersplace.org/what-we-do):

1. **Supportive Housing** — "Provide a safe and stable living environment for men who are experiencing homelessness, addiction and mental health challenges."
2. **Support** — "Offer community case management and linkage to wrap around services to help men overcome barriers they may be facing."
3. **Spirituality** — "Provide spiritual guidance and encourage men to grow in their faith."
4. **Reintegration** — "Help men transition back into society by offering job training and employment opportunities, as well as assistance with securing permanent housing."

V2 has expanded this into five program pages including a `/programs/second-step-program/` that does NOT appear anywhere on the live site. See "Factual inaccuracies" below.
